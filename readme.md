VideoTube
Project Summary
VideoTube is a short video platform designed for entertainment purposes. It allows users to showcase their talents through creative video content. The platform aims to provide a fun and engaging environment for users to express themselves and connect with others.

Features
User-friendly interface
Upload and share short videos
Discover and follow talented creators
Engage with content through likes and comments
